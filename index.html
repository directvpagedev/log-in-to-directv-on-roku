How Do I Log In to DIRECTV on Roku?

Want to stream DIRECTV on your Roku device? You're in luck! DIRECTV Stream is available on most Roku streaming sticks, Roku TVs, and Roku Ultra devices. This guide walks you through the full process of downloading the app, activating it, and logging into your DIRECTV account on Roku.

Is DIRECTV Stream Available on Roku?

Yes! The DIRECTV Stream app is compatible with most Roku models. Whether you have a Roku TV or a streaming stick, you can enjoy full access to DIRECTV Stream with just a few clicks.

Step-by-Step: Log In to DIRECTV on Roku

  1. Turn on your Roku device and ensure it’s connected to the internet.
  2. Go to the Roku Home screen and select “Search”.
  3. Type in “DIRECTV Stream” and select it from the list.
  4. Choose “Add Channel” to install the app.
  5. Once installed, open the DIRECTV Stream app.
  6. Select “Sign In”. You will be given an activation code.
  7. On a browser (mobile or desktop), visit: Official Website
  8. Enter your DIRECTV login credentials and input the Roku activation code.
  9. Click “Activate” — your Roku will refresh and log you in automatically.

What You Need to Sign In

  • A valid DIRECTV account (email and password)
  • Stable internet connection on both Roku and phone/computer
  • The latest version of the Roku OS and DIRECTV Stream app

Forgot Your Login Info?

If you can't remember your password or username, simply go to the login page and click “Forgot Password?” or “Forgot User ID?”. Follow the steps sent to your registered email or phone number to reset your credentials.

Compatible Roku Models

DIRECTV Stream works on these Roku devices and more:

  • Roku Express and Express 4K
  • Roku Streaming Stick models
  • Roku Ultra
  • Roku Premiere
  • Roku Smart TVs (TCL, Hisense, etc.)

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Ensure you’re connected to Wi-Fi with good speed
  • Restart the Roku device if the app crashes
  • Uninstall and reinstall the DIRECTV Stream app
  • Make sure you’re entering the correct login details
  • Try a different device to complete activation if needed

Benefits of Using DIRECTV on Roku

Streaming DIRECTV via Roku provides:

  • Convenient access to live TV and sports
  • Ability to pause, rewind, and record programs
  • Access to premium channels like HBO, STARZ, and more
  • No need for a satellite dish or set-top box
